About Me

I grew up in the Bay Area dancing, swimming, and playing soccer which is what makes me so passionate about movement. Along the way, I had injuries in my shoulder and lower back but never had a full discharge from traditional Physical Therapy because insurance would cut my visits as I was deemed "medically functional." But something felt unfinished. I was still scared to play sports or even pick up my own suitcase. Then, I became too busy as I entered my doctorate program and the workforce. After 2 years of working in insurance based clics, I left the traditional medical system because that unfinished feeling kept creeping up but this time it wasn't with me, it was with the people I was helping. For that reason, I've build Encore Performance that will focus on helping you reach your full potential. My mission is to have a multi-faceted approach on your lifestyle, mental health, and goals. That's what gets me out of bed every morning!

Shreya Wadhawan
